Since last `/compact` (`Ref: pending`), bond-break redistribution was refactored to use direct `OrbitalRedistribution.BuildOrbitalRedistribution` animations from `CovalentBond` instead of `AtomFunction.CoLerpBondBreakRedistribution`, with scope rules: run both fragments for single-bond cleavage and only the heavier side for π/multi-bond break cases. Guide resolution now uses substituent mass across a bonded pair edge when atoms are in the same connected molecule, and tied masses prioritize the OP-side parent atom.

`OrbitalRedistribution` was updated to use standard atomic weights consistently, apply OP-empty handling differently for σ vs π bonding, and preserve operation-path-aware guide behavior so the resolved guide group stays pinned while other rows rotate. `SigmaBondFormation` also switched phase-1 naming/usage and now routes π phase-1 redistribution through its dedicated builder path.
