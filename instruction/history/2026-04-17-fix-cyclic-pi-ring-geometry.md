Since the last compact, cyclic redistribution handling was generalized so cycle-involved atoms automatically populate `ChainRedistributionBlockedAtoms` even when callers do not provide a prebuilt cyclic context. The pi-bond phase-1 path now includes a cyclic coplanarity track for operation atoms, with ring-aware plane selection that can use an existing ring double-bond plane defined by the double-bond pair plus cycle center. Coplanarity motion was refined so movement remains normal to the selected plane while allowing unequal per-atom displacement, and debugging instrumentation added during triage was removed after validation.
